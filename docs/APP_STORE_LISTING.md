## Description

**LeafID** turns your iPhone into a pocket botanist. Point the camera at a leaf, flower, or tree — or pick a photo from your library — and get a clear identification with context you can save, revisit, and share.

Built for hikers, gardeners, students, and anyone who slows down for the plants along the path, LeafID keeps your discoveries organized without losing the joy of being outside.

**What you can do**

- **Identify from camera or photos** — Fast, guided capture with results tuned for real-world lighting and angles.
- **Save to your Herbarium** — Build a personal collection of specimens with notes and imagery you control.
- **See the Arboretum map** — Visualize geotagged finds (when location is available) as pins on an interactive map.
- **Track your Druid profile** — Progress, achievements, and scan energy in a passport-style identity screen.
- **Share specimens** — Export highlights when you want to show off a find or send it to a friend.

LeafID is crafted with a cohesive dark “forest glass” interface, haptic feedback on key actions, and layouts tested for long plant names and dense descriptions so nothing feels cramped on small screens.

**Privacy in plain language**

Sign-in uses **Supabase** for authentication. **We do not sell your personal data.** See the in-app or linked privacy policy for details.
